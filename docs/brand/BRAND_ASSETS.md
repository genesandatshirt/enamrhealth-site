# Enamr Health Brand Assets & Identity

## Brand summary
- **Brand name:** Enamr Health
- **Tagline:** What if your health started with your mouth?
- **Mission (short):** A digital oral health platform translating biomarkers and insights into action.
- **Voice:** Calm, clinical, reassuring, modern.

## Logo
- **Primary wordmark:** ENAMR HEALTH (all caps, letter‑spaced)
- **Usage:** Top-left header and footer wordmark.
- **Clear space:** 1x the height of the letter "E".
- **Minimum size:** 120px width.
- **Do not:** Stretch, add drop shadows, change letter spacing, or alter color.

## Typography
- **Display / Brand:** Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body / UI:** Inter
- **Weights used:** 400, 500, 600

## Color palette
- **Primary dark:** `#0F172A` (Slate 900)
- **Primary text:** `#111827` (Slate 900)
- **Secondary text:** `#64748B` (Slate 500)
- **Background:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Muted background:** `#F8FAFC` (Slate 50)
- **Overlay:** `rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.7)`
- **CTA:** `#0F172A` on white or `#FFFFFF` on dark

## Imagery
- **Style:** Warm, candid, natural light, health‑oriented.
- **Hero:** Video of a person brushing teeth in a calm, modern bathroom.
- **Product/kit:** Clean tabletop shots, soft shadows, blue accent packaging.

## UI patterns
- **Buttons:** Pill shape, solid dark background, white text.
- **Inputs:** Rounded pill, subtle border, light background.
- **Cards:** Soft rounded corners, subtle shadows.
- **Cookie banner:** Bottom‑center, card with subtle shadow.

## Accessibility
- Maintain 4.5:1 contrast for text.
- Ensure CTAs remain legible over video background.
- Provide readable fallback poster for hero video.
